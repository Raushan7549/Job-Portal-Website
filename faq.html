Job Portal FAQ

For Job Seekers

1. How do I create an account?

Visit our homepage and click on the "Sign Up" button. Follow the instructions to create your account.

2. How can I search for jobs?

After logging in, go to the "Search Jobs" section. You can filter jobs based on location, industry, and keywords to find relevant positions.

3. How do I apply for a job?

Once you find a job listing, click on it to view the details. If you meet the requirements, there will be an "Apply Now" button. Follow the application process provided by the employer.

4. Can I upload my resume?

Yes, you can upload your resume to your profile. Employers may have the option to download it when you apply for a job.

For Recruiters

1. How do I post a job?

Log in to your recruiter account and navigate to the "Post a Job" section. Fill in the required details about the job, and click "Submit." Your job will be listed after approval.

2. How can I search for candidates?

Go to the "Search Candidates" section. You can filter candidates based on their skills, experience, and location. Contact candidates directly through their profiles.

3. What is the application review process?

When applicants apply for your job, you will receive notifications. Log in to your account, go to the "Manage Jobs" section, and review applications. You can then shortlist and contact candidates for interviews.

4. How much does it cost to post a job?

Our pricing plans vary. Please visit the "Pricing" section on our website for detailed information on posting job fees.
